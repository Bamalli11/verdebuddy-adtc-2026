CORRECT HAUSA RESPONSES FOR COMMON FARMING QUESTIONS

Q: Yaushe zan shuka masara a Kano?
A: A Kano, lokacin shuka masara na farko shi ne watan Maris zuwa Afrilu. Lokacin na biyu kuma shi ne watan Yuli zuwa Agusta. Ka tabbata kasa tana da danshi mai kyau kafin ka shuka. Yi amfani da nau'in SAMMAZ 15 ko SAMMAZ 27 saboda sun fi dacewa da yankin arewa.

Q: Wane taki ya fi kyau don masara?
A: Don masara, yi amfani da NPK 15-15-15 a lokacin shuka. Bayan makonni shida, kara da Urea don inganta girman hatsi. Adadin da ake bukata shi ne kilogiram 200-300 a kowace hekta.

Q: Yaya zan inganta kasan noma?
A: Don inganta kasa, ka kara da takin gargajiya kamar takin shanu ko takin kaji. Ka yi amfani da takin kwampost kilogiram 5,000-10,000 a kowace hekta. Idan kasa tana da acidity mai yawa, ka kara da lemun kasa (lime) ton 1-2 a hekta.

Q: Yaya zan kare gonar daga kwari?
A: Don kare gona daga kwari, ka yi amfani da maganin kwari da aka yarda da shi. Ka duba gonar kullum don ganin alamun cutuka da wuri. Ka yi jujjuyawar amfanin gona kowace shekara don rage cututtuka.

Q: Menene farashin masara a kasuwa?
A: Farashin masara ya bambanta da lokaci. Bayan girbi (Oktoba-Disamba), farashin kan kasance naira 18,000-25,000 a kowane jaka na kilogiram 100. A lokacin karancin (Afrilu-Agusta), farashin kan kai naira 35,000-55,000. Mafi kyawun lokacin sayarwa shi ne Afrilu zuwa Yuli.

Q: Wadanne nau'in dawa ne suka fi dacewa da yankin arewa?
A: A yankin arewa, nau'in dawa da suka fi dacewa sun hada da SAMSORG 14, SAMSORG 17, da SK 5912. Wadannan nau'i sun jure fari kuma suna samar da yawan kaka mai kyau.
